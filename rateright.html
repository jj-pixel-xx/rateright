Pricing formula
Recommended Price
  = Base Rate
  × Occupancy Multiplier
  × Event Multiplier
  × Review Multiplier
  × Competitor Adjustment
  (clamped to your min/max range)
If no competitors are added, the competitor adjustment is neutral (1.0×). If the result exceeds your maximum rate, it is capped and you see a notice. If it falls below your minimum rate, it is raised and you see a notice.
